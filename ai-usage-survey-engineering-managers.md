This survey helps understand how Engineering Managers are using AI, where it creates value, and what blockers or risks exist.

- Please answer based on your current team and your own workflow.

## Optional Scoring Framework (for survey owner)
Use this after collecting responses:
- Adoption score: Q8 + Q9 + breadth from Q5
- Impact score: average of Q13 + evidence from Q14/Q15
- Readiness score: Q17 + Q18 - blockers from Q19

This helps segment teams into:
- Early exploration
- Emerging adoption
- Scaled adoption
